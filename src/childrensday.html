CHILDREN'SDAY

  • Topic:

    CHILDREN'SDAY

  • Location:

    #H.N:48-62, Papaiah Yadav Nagar, Behind IDPL Colony, Hyderabad-37

  • Date:

    November 14, 2018

Event Description

Children's Day celebrated At Rainbow school on 14th Nov 2019. The day was filled with lots of fun, laughter, happy memories, smiles. The purpose of conducting the competitions was not only to blend learning with fun but also to develop confidence in the students by giving them the opportunity to get on the stage and speak in front of the audience.

Children sang songs and rhymes about the seven colours. All the activities of Rainbow Day were theme based. All their attires resembled the colour of rainbow. The main objective was to deliver the concept of “Rainbow colours” to the children through hands on activity. Rainbow day was made memorable with fun and colours.

In India, Children’s Day is celebrated on 14th November every year to commemorate the birth anniversary of our first Prime Minister, Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru. He loved children dearly and is fondly remembered as Chacha Nehru by them.

After his death in 1964, his birthday was declared as Children’s Day nationwide.

Rainbow school, a centre of excellence in education, believes that every child has a treasure within. We pride ourselves in empowering students to gain Knowledge on various aspects of academics and beyond.
