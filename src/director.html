Our School is a symbol of progressive and quality education.

Rainbow High School is a wonderful place for students from diverse backgrounds to grow educationally, get involved, and have a meaningful high school experience. In every way that schools are measured, whether it is academically, in the fine and performing arts, or athletically, Rainbow High School excels.
